## Understanding the Workspace Concept

A workspace in Robonito is a core organizational unit designed to streamline project management and enhance team collaboration. It serves as a dedicated area for your automation projects, ensuring that tests, configurations, and resources remain perfectly organized and isolated. 

Workspaces enable seamless teamwork, support custom test execution plans, and allow for distinct integrations—providing an incredibly efficient way to manage all of your automation efforts.

## Creating a Workspace

Upon your first login, you will be required to create an initial workspace to house your automation projects.

![Workspace Creation Prompt](/workspace/CreateNewWorkSpace.png)

### Steps to Create:

1. **Access Workspace Creation:** 
   - New users will be automatically prompted upon logging in. 
   - Returning users can simply click the dropdown menu located next to the Robonito logo.
2. **Enter Workspace Name:** Provide a clear, descriptive name and click **Create**.

After successfully creating the workspace, you will be redirected to your dashboard. From there, you can easily create additional workspaces as your organization grows, as illustrated below.

![Dashboard Workspace Dropdown](/test-case/workspace.png)

## Workspace Management

Once your workspace is set up, you can invite team members to collaborate. Key management features include:

- **Logical Separation:** Easily keep unrelated projects and environments organized and isolated.
- **Suite Management:** Link specific test suites directly to the appropriate workspaces.
- **Integrations:** Configure tools and plugins independently for each workspace.

**Key Benefits:** Collaborate effortlessly, stay organized, manage resources efficiently, and scale your testing architecture with ease.
