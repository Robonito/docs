## Understanding Email Integration

Integrating Robonito with your email account via the **IMAP** protocol allows the automation engine to securely read your incoming emails. This feature is incredibly useful for automating test cases that require **One-Time Passwords (OTPs)** or authentication links that are exclusively sent via email. 

Instead of pausing tests or relying on manual intervention, Robonito can automatically grab the necessary data directly from your inbox and inject it seamlessly into your active test workflows.

---

## Setting Up Email Integration

To securely connect Robonito with your email inbox, you will need to provide your account's exact **IMAP server details and encrypted credentials**.

### Configuration Steps:

1. **Go to Integrations:** In your main Robonito dashboard, click on **Integrations** located in the left sidebar.
2. **Select Email:** Locate the Email integration tile and click the `Connect` button to begin the setup process.
3. **Enter Connection Details:** A side drawer configuration form will appear. You must provide the following standard IMAP information:
   - **Email Address:** The specific email account inbox Robonito will connect to.
   - **Password:** Your email account's secure password (or an active **App Password** if 2FA is enabled proactively on your email provider).
   - **IMAP Server:** Your email provider's precise IMAP server address (e.g., `imap.gmail.com`, `mail.yourdomain.com`).
   - **IMAP Port:** The IMAP server's port number (this is typically `993` for secure SSL/TLS connections).
4. **Connect:** After accurately filling in all the required fields, click the `Connect` button. A system notification will actively confirm the success or failure of the connection attempt.
5. **Confirm Integration:** Back on the Integrations page, the Email integration status will securely update to display as `Connected` for your workspace.

## Reading OTPs from Email

> **Important:** Your Email (IMAP) integration must be fully connected and actively verified before attempting to fetch an OTP during a test.

Once your Email integration is successfully set up, you can effortlessly fetch incoming OTPs directly from your emails during a live test case execution.

### Execution Steps:

1. **Start Test Case:** Select the specific test case where you need to read an email OTP and begin recording your automation steps.
2. **Navigate to OTP Page:** During your active recording, naturally navigate to the application screen that strictly requires the OTP verification code.
3. **Fetch OTP:** Once the OTP screen renders, locate your Robonito recording toolbar and click the **Fetch OTP** action button.
4. **Select Source:** An interaction dialog box will instantly appear. From the available options, select **Email** as your target OTP source.
5. **Enter Mail Subject:** In the provided configuration field, enter the exact (or partial) subject line of the email that will contain the OTP. This explicitly helps Robonito filter securely and locate the correct email locally.
6. **Auto-Fill Detection:** Once configured, Robonito will automatically detect the incoming OTP string from the targeted email, locate the input field on your current active page, and instantly populate it with the fetched data.
