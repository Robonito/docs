# Browser Management

The **Browser Management** section allows you to seamlessly download, install, and manage the browsers used for test recording and execution within the Robonito environment. Please note that only browsers explicitly downloaded or installed on your system are actively available for automated test runs.

---

## Accessing Browser Management

Follow the steps below to navigate to the Browser Management page:

1. Log in to your Robonito workspace.
2. Click on your **Profile** icon located in the top-right corner of the interface.  
   ![Profile Icon](/test-case/profile.png)
3. Select **Settings** from the expanding profile menu.
4. Click on the **Browser** tab.  
   ![Browser Settings Menu](/test-case/setting.png)

The Browser Management page will open, providing you full control over all supported browsers for test execution.

---

## About Browsers in Robonito

Robonito utilizes highly-performant, portable browser versions for its automated testing framework:

- **Local Storage:** Portable browsers such as **Google Chrome**, **Mozilla Firefox**, and **Apple Safari** are downloaded locally and securely managed by the Robonito runtime.
- **System Detection:** **Microsoft Edge** is uniquely installed system-wide by Playwright. If it is already available on your machine, Robonito will detect it automatically.
- **Availability:** Only downloaded or system-installed browsers are authorized and available for active recording sessions and test executions.

---

## Supported Browsers Overview

Robonito currently supports the following standard browsers:

![Supported Browsers Downloads Interface](/test-case/browser-downloads.png)

### 1. Google Chrome
**Default Status:** `Downloaded`
- Fully ready for immediate use during both test recording and batch execution.
- Can be manually removed from your system if no longer required for your testing scope.

### 2. Mozilla Firefox
**Default Status:** `Not Installed`
- Must be explicitly requested and downloaded before it can be used for automation.
- **To Download:** Simply click the **Download** button next to Mozilla Firefox. After the backend process finishes, Firefox automatically becomes available for your test execution pipelines.

### 3. Microsoft Edge
**Default Status:** `System Installed`
- Automatically detected and available if it is securely installed on your host operating system.
- No additional local downloads within the Robonito client are required.

---

## Managing Your Browsers

### Downloading a New Browser
- Click the **Download** button located directly next to the required browser name.
- *Note:* Initial download times may vary based on your local internet configuration and speeds.
- Once completed, the browser status officially changes to **Downloaded**.

### Removing an Existing Browser
- Click the **Delete (Trash)** icon next to any actively downloaded browser.
- The browser codebase is safely removed and wiped from the local host system.
- Removed browsers immediately disappear from your active browser selection dropdowns.

---

## Browser Availability During Execution

Only browsers officially marked as **Downloaded** or **System Installed** can be actively configured and selected during the following workflow stages:
- Active Test Recording
- Active Test Execution

Browsers that are marked as `Not Installed` simply will not appear in the test browser selection dropdown menus until they are fully fetched.

---

> **Important Notes:**
> - Initial browser downloads may take several minutes depending heavily on your network footprint and connection conditions.
> - Ensure your local host machine has sufficient available disk space before initiating new browser downloads.
> - **Best Practice:** We highly recommend downloading all your required browser targets simultaneously in advance of setting up new QA testing projects.
