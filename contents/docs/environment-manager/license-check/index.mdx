# License Management

Robonito uses a flexible licensing model to provide its advanced automation features to teams of all sizes. The **License Management** section (accessible via User Settings) allows you to monitor your current subscription status and ensure your automation pipeline remains active.

---

## License Verification

Robonito performs an automatic license check whenever the application is launched. This verification ensures:
- **Validity:** Your license key is active and has not expired.
- **Seat Count:** You are not exceeding the number of concurrent users allowed by your plan.
- **Feature Access:** Advanced modules like Cloud Execution or Mobile Testing are enabled for your account tier.

---

## Troubleshooting License Issues

If your license check fails, the application may enter a "Limited" mode. 

### Common License Errors:

| Error Message | Cause | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| `UNAUTHORIZED` | Invalid or missing license key | Re-enter your key in the License Management menu |
| `EXPIRED` | Your subscription period has ended | Renew your subscription via the Robonito portal |
| `SEAT_LIMIT_REACHED` | Too many active users on one key | Deactivate an unused seat or upgrade your plan |
| `OFFLINE_VERIFICATION_FAILED` | App couldn't ping the license server | Ensure your machine has an active internet connection |

---

## Updating Your License

To update or reactivate your subscription:
1. Navigate to **Settings > License Management**.
2. Click **Update License Key**.
3. Paste your new key and click **Verify**.
4. The application will immediately refresh your feature access status.
