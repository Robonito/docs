### Welcome to Robonito: Revolutionizing Automated Testing

Robonito is an advanced automated testing solution designed to empower teams in delivering high-quality software with remarkable efficiency and accuracy. By automating the testing process, Robonito reduces human error, minimizes manual effort, and accelerates time to market.

Robonito has proven to be an invaluable asset to the Club Car CPQ project, achieving a remarkable reduction in QA testing time by over 70%.
This significant enhancement not only led to substantial cost savings but also greatly expedited the build, testing, and deployment timelines associated with this lead-to-quote digital transformation.

Discover how Robonito can enhance your testing process and enable you to deliver outstanding software experiences.

### Requirements
- Architecture: arm64 or x86_64
- OS: macOS or Windows
- There are no specific disk space or RAM requirements. Robonito smoothly operates with just about 4 GB of RSS RAM, delivering optimal performance without any hassle.
