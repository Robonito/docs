# Navigating Robonito

Robonito is an advanced end-to-end testing platform designed to help QA engineers rapidly automate complex Web, API, and Mobile workflows without needing to architect their own automation infrastructure.

By utilizing Robonito's native recorder and cloud executor, teams can significantly reduce their testing cycle lifespans, detect bugs earlier, and manage their automation strategies entirely through a single intuitive dashboard.

![Robonito Reports](/quicktour/lv_0_20241120155024.gif) 

---

## Core Capabilities

Robonito unifies several powerful testing tools:

- **[Multi-Platform Recording](../web-testing):** Connect your app and generate human-readable test steps simply by clicking through your web pages or mobile emulator.
- **[Test Execution](../executions):** Execute your automated regression suites instantly on your local hardware or dispatch them to Robonito's scalable cloud cluster.
- **[Data Mapping](../data-source):** Organize testing datasets inside CSV files and dynamically inject parameters directly into your test node steps using syntax like `{{@username}}`.
- **[Advanced Integrations](../integrations):** Link your execution suites directly into CI/CD hooks (GitHub Actions) or bug pipelines (Jira).
- **[Scheduled Automations](../schedules):** Run nightly builds automatically using a robust cron schedule parser.
