# User Settings & Profile

The **User Settings** dashboard is your personal control center in Robonito. Here you can manage your account profile, track your workspace usage, and configure security keys for external API access.

---

## 1. Usage & Billing
Track your current workspace consumption against your plan limits:
- **Execution Minutes:** Total time spent running tests in the cloud.
- **Parallel Threads:** Number of concurrent executions allowed.
- **Data Retention:** How long your test reports and videos are stored.

![Usage and Billing dashboard showing execution minutes, parallel threads, and data retention](/environment-manager/env-manager-billing.png)

---

## 2. External APIs (API Keys)
Robonito allows you to generate **API Access Tokens** to interact with our platform programmatically. Use these tokens for:
- **CI/CD Triggers:** Triggering Robonito executions from Jenkins, GitLab, or custom scripts.
- **Report Extraction:** Exporting test data to third-party BI tools.
- **Workspace Management:** Programmatic creation of suites and test cases.

> **Security Note:** Treat your API keys like passwords. Never commit them to version control.

---

## 3. License Management
View and update your Robonito subscription details.
- **Active Plan:** (e.g., Free, Professional, Enterprise)
- **Expiry Date:** When your current cycle ends.
- **Renewal:** Update your payment method or upgrade your tier directly from this menu.

---

## 4. Mobile & Browser Setup
The User Settings sidebar also provides quick access to your local testing environment configurations:
- **[Browser Management](/docs/environment-manager/browsers):** Download and manage Chrome, Firefox, and Edge binaries.
- **[Mobile Setup](/docs/environment-manager/mobile-setup):** Configure Appium and Android/iOS SDKs for local emulation.

---

> **Tip:** You can change your display name and update your avatar in the Profile section at the top of the settings page.
